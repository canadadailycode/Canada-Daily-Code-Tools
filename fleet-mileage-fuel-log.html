Not tax or legal advice. This tool organizes the mileage and fuel-purchase records carriers need for interprovincial fuel tax (IFTA-style) reporting. It does not calculate the tax you owe or are owed — fuel tax rates change every quarter and differ by jurisdiction and fuel type, so any number this tool invented would risk being wrong. It gives you a clean, organized summary by jurisdiction to hand to your accountant or IFTA processing agent, who applies the current official rates. Confirm everything with a licensed accountant before filing.
