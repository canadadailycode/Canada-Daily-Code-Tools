Not business, legal, or professional advice. This is a simple organizing tool for recording customer feedback in your own words. It doesn't analyze, score, or interpret what customers say for you — that judgment stays with you. Confirm any business decision with people you trust before acting on it.
Before you log anything: ask one real customer what they actually dislike, or what almost stopped them from buying. Write down what they said — not your explanation of why they're wrong, not the context, just what they said. You can add your own reflection afterward, but the quote goes in first, unedited.
